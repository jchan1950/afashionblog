Fashion Designing

Having a clear idea of what your wanting to design always helps.

You could write down exactly what you want to get across in your design, then play around with the design untill you get what you think is a great design.

Forgetting your ideas can be really frustrating, so it’s important to make a note of your idea while it’s fresh in your mind, even if it’s just recorded quickly on your phone or scribbled on a scrap of paper.

You’ll be glad of the reminder later when you return to continue working on the design.

As obvious as it may sound, some of history’s greatest designs have come from personal inspirations, with artists drawing on real-life events and traumas to spark their creativity.

Whether you’ve been through hard times or great times, you can use your life experiences to great affect.

Put those feelings into a design you can be proud to show.
